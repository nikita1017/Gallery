Moonlit night on the Dneper

Arkhip Kuindzhi

The painting depicts a summer night. A wide river — the Dnieper — flows calmly among the banks that are soft in
relief. The dark firmament, spread over the earth's surface, creates the impression of infinity. The greenish ribbon of
the river crosses the plain, which merges at the horizon with a dark sky covered with rows of light clouds. In
the gap formed between the clouds, the full moon shines, the light of which is reflected on the water surface.
